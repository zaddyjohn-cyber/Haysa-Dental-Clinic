Our Story

A Decade of Brighter Smiles

Smile Best Dental Clinic was founded in 2015 with one mission: to make expert dental care accessible to every family in Ikeja and Lagos.

Today, with a 28,000-strong community on Instagram and thousands of smile transformations, we remain committed to that same mission — delivering excellence, one smile at a time. Our multidisciplinary team of orthodontists, endodontists, periodontists, and cosmetic dentists work seamlessly together under one roof.
